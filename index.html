Risk Assessment Parameters

Impact Scale: Consider potential consequences if the AI system fails or produces incorrect results. Focus on patient safety, regulatory compliance, and business continuity.
Likelihood Scale: Assess the probability of the AI system experiencing issues based on complexity, data quality, validation extent, and operational environment.
AI Amplifier: This factor accounts for the inherent risks associated with different AI technologies, interpretability challenges, and validation maturity.
